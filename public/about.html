About Jorge

Hey, I'm Jorge

I'm a lot of things. It's often difficult to describe myself, because I don't necessarily think I neatly fit into any one bucket. But here I'll try.

I was the first generation in my family to learn English, live in the US, attend university, and have any sort of career for myself. Here's a picture of me with my parents.

They taught me all the important things in life. They taught me what they could. How to love, how to work, that I should study, and instilled in me the desire to want more for myself and my future.

In all honestly, this is probably all for them. They probably won't use much of what I build, or even understand it. But the fact remains that I know they're deeply proud of my accomplishments and capabilities and are so happy I have set a great foundation for the rest of my life.
